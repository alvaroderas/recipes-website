Hot Dog

Description

These air fryer hot dogs in toasted buns turn out perfectly crisp in minutes with the help of your air fryer. Top with ketchup, mustard, relish, chili, or condiments of choice

Ingredients

  • 1 hot dog bun
  • 1 hot dog
  • Your condiment(s) of choice!

Steps

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Place bun in the air fryer basket; cook in the preheated air fryer until crisp, about 2 minutes. Remove bun to a plate.
  3. Place hot dog in the air fryer basket; cook for 3 minutes. Serve hot dogs in toasted buns and enjoy!
